About
Huang Yingwen

Huang Yingwen was born in Shenzhen, China, in 1995. She graduated from the Open Media Lab of the School of Inter-Media Art, China Academy of Art, and MFA at GAP, Tokyo University of the Arts. Her work is based on an interest in virtual worlds, using various media such as VR, AR, game engines, and audio-visual to explore the relationship between virtual worlds and the world of physical reality. The digital virtual is not the antithesis or anthropomorphism of reality, but perhaps a second reality established through a highly distilled systematic language. And to a certain extent, it reflects the influence and connection between technology, society, and people.
